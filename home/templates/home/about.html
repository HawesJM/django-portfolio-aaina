Aaina Bhargava


Aaina is an arts writer and editor raised and based in Hong Kong. She is currently the Arts and Culture Editor of Tatler Asia, where she continues to identify topical issues and chart growing cultural trends in Asia and around the world. Through interviews with prominent industry names and notable emerging talents, Aaina seeks to create stimulating and meaningful editorial content, covering cultural developments through a relevant, global perspective. Previously a culture reporter at the South China Morning Post, Aaina also contributes to Art Review, The Art Newspaper, Ocula, Design Anthology, and Artomity, and has extensive experience in the art world working for and with a variety of institutions in Asia and the UK.
